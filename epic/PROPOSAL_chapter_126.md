# PROPOSAL: Chapter 126 - "The Great Cooling"

**STATUS**: PROPOSAL DRAFT - NOT WRITTEN
**Timeline**: Oligocene-Miocene (~34-5 MYA)
**Location**: Global climate transition, grassland expansion
**Context**: Antarctic ice sheet formation, mammalian diversification, primate evolution, savanna emergence

## Major Evolutionary Events
### Climate Transition
- **Antarctic glaciation** beginning ice age conditions
- **Global cooling** ending greenhouse world of early Cenozoic
- **Seasonal variation** increasing as climate becomes more variable
- **Ocean circulation** changes affecting global weather patterns

### Grassland Revolution
- **C4 grasses** evolving efficient photosynthesis
- **Savanna expansion** creating new ecological opportunities
- **Grazing mammals** adapting to grass-eating lifestyle
- **Predator-prey** coevolution in open habitats

### Primate Radiation
- **Old World monkeys** diversifying in Africa and Asia
- **New World monkeys** isolated evolution in South America
- **Ape evolution** early hominoids in Africa
- **Brain expansion** increasing intelligence in primate lineages

### Mammalian Specialization
- **Ungulate radiation** horses, rhinos, pigs, ruminants diversifying
- **Carnivore evolution** cats, dogs, bears, weasels specializing
- **Elephant evolution** proboscidean gigantism and intelligence
- **Rodent success** becoming most diverse mammalian order

## Key Characters/Lineages
### The Grass Eaters
- **Mesohippus** - The Middle Horse, three-toed browser to grazer
- **Paraceratherium** - The Near Horn Beast, largest land mammal ever
- **Archaeotherium** - The Ancient Beast, giant omnivorous pig
- **Merycoidodon** - The Ruminant Tooth, early ruminant success

### The Great Apes
- **Proconsul** - The Before Consul, early ape in Africa
- **Sivapithecus** - The Siva Ape, early orangutan ancestor
- **Dryopithecus** - The Tree Ape, European great ape
- **Oreopithecus** - The Mountain Ape, swamp-dwelling Italian ape

### The Predator Guild
- **Amphicyon** - The Ambiguous Dog, bear-dog hybrid predator
- **Smilodon** - The Knife Tooth, saber-tooth cat perfection
- **Megalictis** - The Great Weasel, giant mustelid carnivore
- **Borophagus** - The Gluttonous Eater, bone-crushing dog

### The Proboscideans
- **Deinotherium** - The Terrible Beast, downward-curved tusk elephant
- **Gomphotherium** - The Bolt Beast, four-tusked elephant ancestor
- **Platybelodon** - The Flat Spear Tooth, shovel-tusked elephant
- **Stegodon** - The Roof Tooth, straight-tusked Asian elephant

### The Marine Mammals
- **Livyatan** - The Leviathan, giant sperm whale predator
- **Carcharocles** - The Jagged Tooth, megalodon shark ancestor
- **Odobenocetops** - The Walrus Whale, bizarre whale with tusks
- **Desmostylus** - The Bond Pillar, hippo-like marine mammal

## Themes
- Climate change driving mammalian evolution and adaptation
- Grassland expansion creating new ecological opportunities
- Primate intelligence evolution in African environments
- Mammalian body size experimentation and specialization
